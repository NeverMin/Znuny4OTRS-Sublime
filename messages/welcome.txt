Znuny4OTRS is a Sublime Text 2/3 plugin that helps you to make OTRS development less painful.

You have to do nothing now. Just check out the list of fancy shortcuts in your preferences or type "otrs" and trigger sublime autocompletion.

New features are comming up!
GitHub link - https://github.com/znuny/Znuny4OTRS-Sublime

For this extension and for OTRS in general visit http://znuny.com. Looking forward to hear from you!

Enjoy!
